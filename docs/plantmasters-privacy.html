Plant Masters - Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 17, 2026

Neuroplastic Labs ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Plant Masters mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect your email address and password. Passwords are securely hashed and stored via our authentication provider (Supabase). We never store passwords in plain text.

Profile Information

You may optionally provide a nickname, bio, and location for your public profile. You control whether your location is visible to other users.

Plant Photos

When you use our plant identification and diagnosis features, photos you capture or select are sent to our servers for AI analysis. Photos are processed to generate plant care reports and are stored in your analysis history.

Location Data

With your permission, we collect approximate location data to connect you with local plant communities and provide region-specific plant care recommendations. You can deny or revoke location access at any time in your device settings.

Usage and Analytics

We collect anonymized usage data to improve the app experience, including feature usage patterns, app performance metrics, and crash reports. We use third-party services (RevenueCat, Superwall) to manage subscriptions and optimize the user experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide plant identification, diagnosis, and care recommendations
  • To manage your account and subscription
  • To enable community features (posts, comments, groups)
  • To improve app performance and user experience
  • To send important service-related communications

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with:

  • Service providers: Supabase (database and authentication), OpenAI (plant analysis AI), RevenueCat (subscription management), Superwall (paywall optimization)
  • Other users: Your public profile information and community posts are visible to other Plant Masters users, as controlled by your privacy settings
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect the rights and safety of our users

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase with Row Level Security (RLS) policies ensuring users can only access their own data. Authentication tokens are stored in your device's Apple Keychain. All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Update or correct your information
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Opt out of location data collection
  • Control the visibility of your profile information

6. Children's Privacy

Plant Masters is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected data from a child under 13, we will delete it promptly.

7. Subscriptions

Plant Masters offers optional in-app subscriptions (Pro and Master tiers) managed through Apple's App Store and RevenueCat. Payment processing is handled entirely by Apple. We do not collect or store credit card information.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
